Frequently Asked Questions

What is CryptoPilot?

CryptoPilot is a secure and user-friendly platform for buying, selling, and managing a wide range of cryptocurrencies. We provide tools for both beginners and experienced traders.

How do I open an account?

You can open an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" button and following the simple registration steps. You will need to complete a quick verification process (KYC) for security and compliance.

Is my money safe with CryptoPilot?

Yes, security is our top priority. We employ industry-leading security measures, including cold storage for the majority of funds, two-factor authentication (2FA), and regular security audits to protect your assets.

What cryptocurrencies can I trade?

CryptoPilot supports over 300 cryptocurrencies, including popular ones like Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), and many altcoins. Our list is constantly expanding.

Are there any fees for trading?

We offer transparent and competitive fees, which vary depending on your trading volume and account tier. Please refer to our Pricing section for detailed information.
